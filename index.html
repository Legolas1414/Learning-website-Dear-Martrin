Dear Martin

English School Project

About the Book

Dear Martin is a novel written by Nic Stone. It was published in 2017.

The story is about Justice McAllister, a Black teenager. He goes to a very good school but still faces racism.

Main Message

The book talks about racism and racial profiling. This means people are judged because of their skin color.

It also shows that behaving well does not always protect Black people from unfair treatment.

Character Web – Focus on Manny

Manny is Justice’s best friend. He is honest and speaks his mind. Manny is angry about racism and does not trust the system.

Manny

• Justice’s best friend
• Very direct and honest
• Angry about racism
• Dies because of police violence

Justice

• Main character
• Good student
• Writes letters to Martin Luther King
• Believes in peaceful change

Sarah-Jane

• Justice’s girlfriend
• White and rich
• Does not see racism at first

Jared

• Classmate
• Says racist things
• Shows everyday racism

Connection to Real Life

The problems in the book are real. Many Black people are stopped by the police because of their skin color.

This is why movements like Black Lives Matter exist.

Martin Luther King Jr.

Justice writes letters to Martin Luther King Jr. He looks up to him.

Martin Luther King fought against racism using peaceful protests. The book asks if this still works today.
